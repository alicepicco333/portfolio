Academic Projects

CAPPellini



CAPPellini is the final project work for the course of Interaction Design of the Digital Humanities Master program at University of Bologna. The project had the goal to create an interactive tour of the geological museum "Giovanni Cappellini" in Bologna. "CAPPellini" is a concept for an interactive mobile application intended for a target of elementary school children either on a school trip or visiting the museum with their families.

Digitalian Folktales

Digitalian Folktales is the final project work for the course of Digital Scholarly Editing of the Digital Humanities Master program at University of Bologna. It's a critical edition inspired by the work "Fiabe Italiane" (Italian Folktales) by Italo Calvino. We searched for the original folktales in the regional languages that Calvino translated to italian and included in the collection. Digitalian Folktales is a tool to visualize the original and the translated folktale at the same time, together with a map that reveals its geographic provenance.
